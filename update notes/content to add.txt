ETEM A KARAMOJA (10:00 AM – 1:00 PM)
Meaning: “The Voice of Karamoja”
Tone: Honest • Cultural • Bold • Community-first
Etem a Karamoja is the heart of community conversation on EKO FM.
Broadcast in Ngakarimojong with English support, the show gives voice to the people—sharing real stories, real challenges, and real solutions from across Karamoja.
Each week, the program dives into key issues affecting daily life, featuring community voices, expert guests, live call-ins, and deep cultural reflection. It is a space where truth is spoken openly, and solutions are explored together.
This is more than talk radio—it is community in action.
